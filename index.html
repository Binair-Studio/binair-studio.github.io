About Us

We are a passionate indie game development team, based on the collaboration of creative minds from different corners of the world. Our heart beats for the development of unique game experiences that inspire players of all ages and transport them to other worlds.

Specializing in Roblox Studio, our goal is to create games that are not only entertaining, but also innovative and educational. Our work is characterized by a deep passion for richly detailed worlds, thoughtful gameplay mechanics and intuitive user interfaces. We strongly believe that every game should tell a story that touches and challenges players at the same time.

Although we have also worked with Unreal Engine 4 in the past, an unexpected loss of our UE4 projects inspired us to invest even more of our resources and creativity into development with Roblox Studio. This challenge has strengthened us as a team and sharpened our focus to continue testing and pushing the boundaries of what is possible on the Roblox platform.

We are currently hard at work on our first major project, a game that pushes the boundaries of Roblox and creates a deep, immersive experience for our players. Every element, from story to design, is being carefully considered and implemented to create a world that is both inviting and challenging.

As a team, we bring a diverse range of skills and experience, from game design and programming to UI/UX design, storytelling and 3D asset modeling. This diversity allows us to bring unique perspectives to our projects and create games that excel both technically and artistically.

We are only at the beginning of our journey and are eager to turn our visions into reality and enrich the gaming world with our creations. Our indie game development team is not just a group of developers - we are dreamers, artists and storytellers dedicated to creating unforgettable gaming experiences.

We combine professional design principles with our passion for gaming to create games that not only entertain, but also inspire and challenge. Our mission is to push boundaries and constantly redefine the expectations of what is possible in the world of game design.

    At the heart of our game UI/UX design work is the creation of intuitive and inviting user interfaces that take the gaming experience to a new level. Our goal is to create seamless and immersive experiences where player interactions are intuitive and fluid, supported by visually stunning and functional designs. We pay great attention to detail and usability to ensure that our games are not only visually appealing, but also user-friendly and inclusive. By constantly exploring new design trends and techniques, we strive to push the boundaries of what is possible in game UI/UX design and create games that are both beautiful and deeply interactive.

Experiences

  1. Experience with Roblox Studio and Blender

    2022 - Present

    Since 2022 we have focused on development with Roblox Studio, complemented by 3D modeling and animation in Blender. This combination has allowed us to create custom assets and give our games a unique visual identity in Roblox. Through Roblox Studio, we have learned how to create games that are both accessible and in-depth, reaching a wide audience. The integration of Blender has further enhanced our ability to create detailed and engaging game worlds, making our projects stand out in the Roblox community.

  2. Experience with Unreal Engine

    2020 - 2022

    After starting with Scratch, we switched to Unreal Engine, one of the most advanced game development platforms. Between 2020 and 2022, we worked intensively with Unreal Engine, which allowed us to gain deep insights into 3D game development, realistic environments and complex gameplay mechanics. During this time, we developed several prototypes and projects that expanded our technical know-how and creative vision, even though these projects no longer exist.

  3. Experience with Scratch

    Early 2020

    Our journey into game development began in early 2020 with Scratch, a visual programming language that allowed us to learn the basics of logic and interactivity in games. Through working with Scratch, we developed a solid understanding of basic programming concepts and the importance of user engagement, which laid the foundation for our further development projects.
